Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026  ·  Jay Air Heating & Cooling Inc

Jay Air Heating & Cooling Inc respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect through this website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Who We Are

Jay Air Heating & Cooling Inc is a licensed HVAC contractor operating in Central Florida (License No. CAC1816530). Our website address is jayairflorida.com. You can contact us at jayairteam@gmail.com or (407) 782-4426.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact/estimate request form. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Description of your HVAC service need

We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit is used solely to:

  • Respond to your service inquiry or estimate request
  • Schedule appointments or follow up on your request
  • Communicate with you about services you requested

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Form Processing — Third-Party Service

Our contact form is processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to our email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services. We do not track your browsing behavior or use any third-party advertising networks.

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill your service request and for standard business record-keeping. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at jayairteam@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights (Florida Residents)

Under the Florida Information Protection Act (FIPA) and applicable federal law, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jayairteam@gmail.com.

8. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) to request access to, deletion of, or opt-out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us directly.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the personal information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Links to Other Websites

This website may contain links to external sites (e.g., Google Maps, Goodman). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
